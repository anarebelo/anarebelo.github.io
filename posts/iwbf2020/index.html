International Workshop on Biometrics and Forensics

The International Workshop on Biometrics and Forensics (IWBF) is devoted to facilitating synergies in research and development among the areas of multimedia forensics, forensic biometrics, and forensic science.

IWBF 2020 is jointly organized by EAB; INESC TEC, Porto, Portugal and NTNU, Gjovik, Norway.

Important dates

  • Papers / Demos / Doctoral Consortium Submission Deadline: February 15, 2020
  • Conference: April 29-30, 2020

For more information please consult the official website.
