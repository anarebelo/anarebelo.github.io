1st Biometric Recognition with Portable Devices Competition

This competition is part of ICIAR 2013 conference and the MobBIO Multimodal Database is used.

The main goal of the competition is to compare different methodologies for biometric recognition using data acquired with common portable devices, such as smartphones or tablets.

The team involved were: Ana F. Sequeira, Hélder Oliveira, João Monteiro and I.
